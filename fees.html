Fee Payment Information

  • Admission Fee: One-time payment at the time of admission (non-refundable)
  • Monthly Fee: Can be paid monthly or annually (10% discount on annual payment)
  • Annual Charges: Includes library, lab, sports, and development charges
  • Other Fees: Includes examination, stationery, and miscellaneous charges
  • Transportation: Rs. 1,500 - Rs. 3,000/month based on distance
  • Late Fee: Rs. 100/month for delayed payment after 10th of each month

Discounts & Scholarships

  • Sibling Discount: 10% on tuition fee for second child
  • Merit Scholarship: Up to 25% discount for top performers
  • Early Payment: 5% discount for annual fee paid before June 1st
  • Financial Aid: Available for deserving students (subject to approval)
